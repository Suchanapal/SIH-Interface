Doppler FrequencyMotion

Microwave Doppler radar emits microwave signals and detects the reflected signals from the target. The frequency of the reflected signal is Doppler shifted due to the relative motion between the radar and the target. A peak in the frequency of the reflected signal indicates that the target is moving towards the radar, which could be a sign that a human is present.
